South America. In the Atlantic, Hippocampus erectus ranges from Nova Scotia to Uruguay. H. zosterae,
known as the dwarf seahorse, is found in the Bahamas.
Colonies have been found in European waters such as the Thames Estuary.
Three species live in the Mediterranean Sea: H. guttulatus (the long-snouted seahorse),
H. hippocampus (the short-snouted seahorse), and H. fuscus (the sea pony). These species
